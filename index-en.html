Appz

Version 1.1.3 04/13/2026
Portuguese

Appz is a macOS launcher built to keep things simple. Open your favorite apps right from the menu bar or your custom Launchpad.

Appz is available on the App Store

Features

Menu bar

Keep your favorite apps right there in the macOS menu bar.

Launchpad

Open Appz from anywhere using a global keyboard shortcut.

Custom groups

Create app groups and organize your Launchpad your way.

Drag and drop

Create new groups or drop apps into groups you already have.

App suggestions

Appz learns from your usage and suggests apps you use most often.

Menu bar icon

Prefer a cleaner look? You can hide the menu bar icon anytime.

Privacy Policy

  1. Data Collection

    Appz does not collect, store, or send any personal data or usage information.

  2. Registration and Identification

    Appz does not require an account, sign-up, or login. Your use of the app is not tied to any personal identity.

  3. Tracking and Cookies

    Appz does not use tracking tools (such as third-party trackers, analytics SDKs, or telemetry). It also does not use cookies or similar technologies to monitor behavior.

  4. Local Processing

    Everything needed for Appz to work (app indexing, search, and launch actions) runs locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers or cloud services.

  5. Third-Party Services

    Appz does not integrate third-party services.
