Appz

Features Help Portuguese

Appz is a macOS launcher focused on simplicity. Access your favorite applications from the menu bar or custom Launchpad.

Features

Menu bar

Keep your favorite apps available on the macOS menu bar.

Launchpad

Open Appz from anywhere with a global shortcut.

Custom groups

Create app groups and organize Launchpad your way.

Drag and drop

Create new groups or add apps to existing groups.

Suggested apps

Appz observes your usage pattern and suggests the apps you use most.

Menu bar icon

Prefer a cleaner UI? Hide the menu bar icon.

Help

General settings

In Appz settings, open General settings and choose whether Appz should open at login, hide the menu bar icon, or customize the global Launchpad shortcut.

Custom folders

Appz searches for installed applications on your Mac, but you can add custom folders containing apps that are not installed on the system.

Choose favorites

Click an app to mark it as a favorite. Click again to unmark it.

Suggestions

Apps used most frequently that were not marked as favorites are suggested by Appz; accept or ignore each suggestion.

Groups

Groups help organize apps in Launchpad. Create a group by dragging one app onto another.

Drag an app onto an existing group to add it.

Groups can be managed through the context menu. Use the options to create, edit, add, or remove apps from groups. A group can be created from an app's context menu.

To edit a group, use the group context menu.
